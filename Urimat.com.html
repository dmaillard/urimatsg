MB-Active Trap

The patent for 100% odour-free function

Protection from Bad Odour and Waste Pipe Clogging
The Integrated MB-ActiveCube binds bad odour (ammonia) within the pipe before it emerges by dissolving organic enzymes.  Reduces deposits, prevents the inside of the odour trap and the waste pipe becoming encrusted with organic matter, urine deposits and fats.
Additional Odour Control
Odours are completely sealed away by the vertical sealing membrane technology. It withstands both negative and positive pressures and makes it impossible for any gases to escape from the sewage system.
Continuous Service
After the MB-ActiveCube has been dissolved, the red surface beneath the transparent cover signals that the odour trap shall be replaced.
Dosage of MB Active Cube
The patented three-way control allows to dose the integrated MB-ActiveCube for optimal effectiveness and service life.
